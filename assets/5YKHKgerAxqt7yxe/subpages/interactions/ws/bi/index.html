How to Use This Tool

  1. List What You Know: Start by listing all the information you already have about your topic. This helps establish a foundation for your brainstorming session.
  2. List What You Want to Know: Identify areas where you need more information or have questions. This guides your research and exploration.
  3. Add Items: Use the input fields under each list to add your points. Press the "Add" button or hit "Enter" to include them in the respective list.
  4. Answer Guiding Questions: After adding an item, a prompt will appear asking you to answer guiding questions related to that item.
