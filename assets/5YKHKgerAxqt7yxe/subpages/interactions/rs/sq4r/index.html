SQ4R Reading Tool

Welcome to the SQ4R Reading Tool

The SQ4R method is a structured approach to reading that enhances comprehension and retention. It stands for:

  • Survey
  • Question
  • Read
  • Recite
  • Relate
  • Review

This tool will guide you through each step of the SQ4R process, helping you engage deeply with your reading material.

Select "Start" to begin the process.
