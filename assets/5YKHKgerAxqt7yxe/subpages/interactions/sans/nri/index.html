Note Review Tool

Step 1: Reorganize or Rewrite Notes

Select one or more formats below to reorganize your notes. Outline for a textual breakdown, Chart to arrange info in rows/columns, or Mind Map for a visual approach. Reorganizing your notes can help you grasp the material more effectively.

Step 2: Create Flashcards

Use flashcards to reinforce memory. Each card has a Term and a Definition. Flashcards are a form of active learning that helps you retain important information better than simply rereading your outline, chart, or mind map. This helps with quick self-testing and spaced repetition.

Step 3: Schedule Review Time

Setting a review date and time increases the chance you'll follow through. Consistent review is key to retaining knowledge long-term.
