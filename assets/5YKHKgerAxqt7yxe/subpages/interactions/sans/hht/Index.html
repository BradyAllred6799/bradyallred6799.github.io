Homework Help Tool

Hello! Welcome to the Homework Help Tool.

This tool will help you break your homework into smaller, manageable chunks and provide guidance to help you reach your goals. Here’s how it works:

  • Work in 25-Minute Blocks: Set a goal for what part of the assignment you will complete in each block. After 25 minutes, review your progress and set a new goal for the next block.
  • Take Breaks: You have the option to take a 5-minute break after each block. After three 25-minute blocks, you can take a longer break.
  • Track Your Goals: We will keep a record of your goals, which you can access at any time. You can also take notes while you work.
  • Reflect and Create a Study Guide: At the end of the session, reflect on what you have learned. Record information to help you create a study guide for your course.
  • Remember, not meeting a goal is not a failure. It just helps you set a more informed goal for the next block.
