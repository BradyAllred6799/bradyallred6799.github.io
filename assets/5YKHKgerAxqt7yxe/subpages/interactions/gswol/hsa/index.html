Help Seeking Assistant

Help-seeking can be a challenging thing, especially if you don’t know where to look for help. This tool will guide you through the 4-step help-seeking process: Become Aware of Needs, Identify Helpers, Ask for Help, and Evaluate the Help.
Remember, recognizing your needs is the first step towards personal growth.

Step 1: Become Aware of Needs

Take a few minutes to identify your most pressing needs. They can be academic or non-academic, but ensure they are genuine and significant.

*Acknowledging your needs is a courageous step towards improvement.*

Step 2: Identify Helpers

For each need you've identified, list the helpers available to you. Helpers can be individuals, organizations, or resources that can assist you.

*Seeking support is a powerful way to overcome challenges and grow.*

Step 3: Ask for Help

For each helper you've identified, create a plan detailing how and when you will reach out to them.

*Taking initiative to ask for help fosters resilience and continuous learning.*

Step 4: Evaluate the Help

After you’ve sought help, return to this plan that you have saved and reflect on your experience. Answer the following questions:

  • Did the resource help me in the way I expected?
  • Do I need more help, or can I move forward on my own?
  • Would I recommend this resource to a friend?
  • Will I use this type of resource again?

*Great job on completing your help-seeking plan! Remember, growth is a continuous journey.*
