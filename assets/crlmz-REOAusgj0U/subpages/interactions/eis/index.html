Eisenhower Matrix

Use the Eisenhower Matrix to prioritize tasks by adding them to the appropriate quadrant based on their urgency and importance. You can drag and drop tasks between quadrants, edit or complete them, and view completed tasks by toggling the "Show Completed Tasks" button. When ready, click the Print button to print a copy of your tasks.
